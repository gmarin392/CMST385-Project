Site Map

The structure and organization of the website will make it easy for customers and viewers to navigate. The organization will have a hierarchy starting from the HOME page, leading the viewers to navigate to the other tabs: Menu, Hours & Location, Contact Us, and Order. From the selected topics, customers can view more details about the specific icon/subfolders.

The website will also incorporate map integration for specific location of the restaurant.
